Foundation

The Gitic Foundation is an open decentralized organization comprised of developers, innovators, investors, builders, and other contributors. Our mission is to collaboratively build open-source projects on various blockchain networks. We are open to all individuals who wish to join and contribute based on their expertise, with the goal of creating a better decentralized future by tight different technologies with power of decentralization.

Join Foundation

  • Introducing Gitic

  • Welcome to Gitic, where the future of decentralized governance is in your hands! Gitic is a groundbreaking project that empowers its citizens to shape its destiny. At Gitic, we believe that true decentralization means putting the power back into the hands of the community.

  • Our unique approach allows Gitic citizens to vote on the future of the project, specifically on whether to adopt a Token System or a Tokenless System. This decision will shape the entire structure and direction of Gitic, influencing how it operates and evolves.

  • Once the voting is complete, the chosen governance system will be implemented, and Gitic will release its full structure, enabling citizens to participate actively in building and shaping the decentralized future we envision. Join us in this historic moment, where your vote will define the path Gitic takes, and together, we will build a more decentralized and democratic future!

  • Why Vote?

  • At Gitic, your vote is your voice in shaping the future of decentralized governance. By participating in the voting process, you play a crucial role in deciding the direction of the Gitic ecosystem. Your vote ensures that the decisions made align with the values and goals of the community. So, why wait? Cast your vote today and be a part of building a decentralized future together!

  • Voting Benefits

  • Voting in Gitic empowers you to have a direct impact on the decisions that shape the future of decentralized governance. Your vote ensures that your voice is heard and that your priorities are represented in the decision-making process. By participating in voting, you become an active contributor to the development and evolution of the Gitic ecosystem. So, seize the opportunity to vote and be a part of building a more inclusive and transparent governance model!

  • Governance

  • Gitic Governance represents an unstoppable decentralized system characterized by its core components - a robust constitution, an annually curated budget, and the diverse framework of states operating across various blockchain networks. This governance structure embodies the essence of decentralized decision-making, fostering an ecosystem driven by collective and inclusive participation. Through Gitic Governance, stakeholders actively contribute to shaping the trajectory of the organization, ensuring a resilient and adaptable foundation for a decentralized and democratic future.
