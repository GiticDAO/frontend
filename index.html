About

Gitic its all about cater to the needs of various stakeholders in the software development ecosystem, including developers, innovators, and investors. The platform focuses on three key aspects: providing frameworks and tools for developers, offering grants for innovators, and facilitating the connection between developers and innovators for the benefit of investors.

Resource management

Resource management refers to the efficient allocation, organization, and management of design resources within a design project or organization. These resources may include design software, tools, hardware, personnel, and other materials necessary to complete a design project successfully.
